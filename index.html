9/29/19 - 8:01 am

The PNW 'gloom' as I like to call it is beginning to roll in. A stirring pot of wet, cold, fog, and grey from now till March - that's the 'gloom.' Seasonal depression is a real thing around here and so it's incredibly important to remain active, as opposed to bundling up in a dark room with Netflix all-day, all-week, all-season...

But on the bright side, at least I get to layer up. I'm excited to wear some jackets and sweaters again.

9/28/19 - 7:33 am

In an earlier post, I mentioned that the first thing I do every morning is I drink a glass of water. I'm gonna change up the routine.

Before the glass of water, the first thing to do is roll the blinds, crack the window open and take in a deep breath of the fresh, morning air - in through the nostrils and out through the mouth. The 6 am air has a minty, healing power to it; it's like toothpaste for the mind. Why wait till I get to the coffee shop to breathe it in? I have the luxury to do so first thing in the morning, so that's what I'll begin to do. Besides, it's only until I step out of my room and walk back in when I realize how muggy it is at times - probably best to brush the junk out ASAP.
